About Us

Learn more about our journey and commitment to beauty

Welcome to Moomal Salon & Training Center

Established with a passion for beauty and excellence, Moomal Salon has been serving the community of Larkana with premium beauty services. Our team of certified professionals is dedicated to bringing out your natural beauty with the latest techniques and highest quality products.

In addition to our salon services, we take pride in our Training Center where we've helped hundreds of students launch successful careers in the beauty industry through our comprehensive courses and hands-on training.

Located near Gurai Hospital, Resham Galli, Larkana, we're easily accessible and committed to providing a luxurious experience for all our clients.
